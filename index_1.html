About the Book

Reading, broken down to three simple shapes.

Carrie Peoples' Reading System is an approachable, playful method built around shapes children already know: circles, lines, and dots. These familiar marks become the building blocks of every letter — and every letter becomes the doorway to a word.

The system is rooted in a simple observation: children naturally use words they can say long before they can recognize them in print. This book bridges that gap. It meets little learners where they are, then gently walks them forward.

More than a phonics workbook, it's a fun, structured journey that builds thinking skills, vocabulary, and a strong foundation in early reading — one shape, one sound, one word at a time.

Meet the Authors

Two educators. One mission.

More than 60 combined years helping children unlock the magic of reading.

CP

Carrie E. Peoples

Author · Veteran Educator

Carrie is a disabled US Navy Veteran whose education career began in the early 1970s as a federal educator. Her decades of service have shaped countless classrooms across the country.

  • ★Staff assistant to President Nixon's Secretary of Education
  • ★Managed Teacher Corps Networks across 122 projects
  • ★Four decades as a public school teacher specializing in Gifted & Talented Education in California and Georgia
